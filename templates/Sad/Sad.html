Analytics

Songs that are appropriate for a sad mood are low in valence and high in acousticness. Songs appropriate for a sad mood must encourage self-reflection and subdued angst. One must feel as if they are on the verge of tears, if not full on bawling. Sad metrics mirror happy metrics, in that they have a slower, more negative feeling overall. We added in high acousticness as we saw a relation between sadness and songs with guitar and louder acoustics.
